About Us

PROGRAMMES OFFERED VIT-AP offers distinctive education through its undergraduate, postgraduate and PhD programmes.

VIT-AP School of Computer Science & Engineering VISION: To be a preeminent school in application-driven education and state-of-the-art research in computer science and engineering that meets the dynamic industrial and societal needs. MISSION: ★ To impart profound knowledge on theoretical and practical concepts in computer science and engineering ★ To provide an academic environment that prepares students with skills required to succeed in their profession and contribute to the society. ★ To create a research ecosystem that encourages faculty and students to collaborate and innovate. ★ To enhance industry–institute collaborations and community outreach programmes.
